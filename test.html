FAQ

Q Why are a player's and heroe's recent matches limited to 8 days?
A The website only shows 8 days because that's the time Valve stores replays. So 8 days after the match has ended, the replay is not available for download anymore.
Q Why are players missing?
A I add players manually. If you're missing someone, send a dotabuff link to me and I will consider adding the player. If you're aware of someone's smurf account and it has not been added yet, I'd appreciate if you tell me that as well (please include proof that it is indeed the account of a notable player).
Q Is your website down?
A Whoops. Sometimes something crashes and either the website is unavailable or matches don't show up. I fix it as soon as I notice it, but it can sometimes take couple of hours for me to notice, especially at night.
